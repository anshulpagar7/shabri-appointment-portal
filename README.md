# 📌 Overview

**ADI SAMPARK** is a comprehensive digital appointment management platform developed for **Adivasi Vikas Bhavan** to simplify appointment scheduling between citizens and the Office of the Commissioner / Managing Director.

The platform digitizes appointment booking, visitor management, executive scheduling, holiday management, official tour management and real-time monitoring through dedicated portals for Citizens, Staff and the Commissioner / Managing Director.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Digitize appointment scheduling
- Reduce citizen waiting time
- Eliminate manual appointment registers
- Improve office workflow
- Enable real-time appointment monitoring
- Provide transparency in queue management
- Improve communication between citizens and administration
